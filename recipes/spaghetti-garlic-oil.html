Spaghetti With Garlic And Oil

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic (sliced thin)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh parsley (chopped, for garnish)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta Bring a pot of salted water to a boil, add spaghetti, and cook according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve about 1/4 cup of pasta water, then drain.
  2. Prepare the garlic and oil In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced garlic and red pepper flakes. Saut√© until the garlic is golden brown and fragrant, but not burnt (about 2 minutes).
  3. Combine pasta and sauce: Add the drained pasta to the pan with garlic and oil. Toss to combine and add a little of the reserved pasta water to help the sauce coat the spaghetti evenly.
  4. Season: Add salt to taste and toss everything together again.
  5. Serve: Plate the pasta, garnish with chopped parsley, and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese if desired.

Enjoy your simple and delicious spaghetti!
